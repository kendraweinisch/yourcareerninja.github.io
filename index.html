Transition FAQ

Common questions about encore careers, fractional roles, and board placement for C-suite leaders

What is an encore career for leaders?

An encore career represents a purposeful transition where accomplished leaders leverage their experience in new ways - through fractional roles, board positions, advisory work, or mission-driven ventures that combine income with impact and personal fulfillment.

How long does transition typically take?

Most C-suite transitions require 6-12 months for meaningful repositioning. The Fractional Launch program accelerates this to 4-8 weeks, while board placement typically spans 4-9 months depending on target roles and preparation level.

What's the difference between fractional and part-time roles?

Fractional leaders typically serve multiple organizations simultaneously with project-based leadership, while part-time roles involve dedicated hours to a single organization. Both offer flexibility but require different positioning and client management approaches.

Do I need specific certifications for board positions?

While not always required, certifications like NACD Directorship Certification significantly enhance credibility and competitiveness for corporate board roles. Our Board-Ready Accelerator includes preparation for these certifications.
